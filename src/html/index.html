Composer and sound-designer

About

My name is Sergei Ikonnikov and I love to create the music. I like to begin my music projects from the entire idea to the last notes. Usually I compose using virtual instruments in digital audio workstation on PC. It's almost true I work in digital only, but:

  • -sometimes I record other people. Usually they are bass/guitar players or signers.
  • -sometimes I record myself. Yeah, Usually I record bass and electric guitar.

Short story

I'm an independent composer and musician from Russia, borned in Novosibirsk city. I've started to learn guitar and play in local bands since 2003. I've started to learn digital recording in 2013 and tried myself as a composer/sound-designer. It was a time when indipendent company called "FateStudio" invited me to create games. That's how I started to create music/soundtracks since august 2013

Passion to music

The first intrument

String music instruments is my passion from the begining. I'm telling about a guitar, ofcourse. Guitar was the first music instrument my hands reached when I grew up. I studied electric guitar the most part of my music life and learned how to play it without any help, except some advices from my friends. But I can't say that I've mastered guitar, I'm too far to become a local professional of guitar. So far so good I can play what I need.

Digital Audio Workstations. First steps.

My first record was published after some years past. The first steps were the most hard, because you never knew how this works or even will it work. All guitar tracks I've recorded were sounding not natural and got noises. I have no music tracks from those times...and I'm glad that I don't have any. New tracks sounds better and I can proud with them.

Becoming a composer

Working in IT industry I've met a lot of good people. Men who founded a a little company of indie developers were one of those good peaple.
I left my work to join them and try myself as a composer and sound-designer, after some time was spent on talks and to made a test tasks. It was a time "I wanted to start from the begining"© so I've spent some time to get some money and leave to my new "work".
"This track is good, but we should move ahead."© Andrew Viktorov
Those words were waiting for me after every track. It was joyful. Company was called "Fate Studio" and our ship couldn't make it.

My first album, and goodbye to "Fate Studio".

Good times couldn't lasts forever. I've reached my limits in money, so I went to the job after some time. But I have a portfolio since those times attached on SoundCloud website and I've learned a lot new. The list of my live instruments were:

I've continued to improve my skill at a free time and passed to another important event of my life. I've decided to record my first full album. It was dedicated to my ex band called "Shine now".
